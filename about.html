ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Kat. I am based in Lexington, KY. I am currently in the process of getting my Master's in Computer Science as well as a graduate certificate in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence from Eastern Kentucky University with an expected graduation date of December 2023.

I received my Bachelor's from Asbury University in Spanish Language & Composition in 2017. Shortly after, I taught English as a foreign language in Gwangju, South Korea until the end of 2018. For the last five years, I have worked for the federal government in dual capacities. From 2018 to 2022, I worked as a Spanish Interpreter and Document Translator for the Social Security Administration. In 2020 I was selected to attend Officer Candidate School for the U.S.Army and have worked as an Operations Officer and Platoon Leader for a transporation company ever since.
